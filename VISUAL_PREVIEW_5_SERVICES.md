## 🎓 Design Philosophy

### Why These Widgets Work:

**MACT - Green Compensation:**
- Color Psychology: Green = money, success, hope
- Information Need: Victims need compensation clarity
- Action: Motivates inquiry

**Property - Services Card:**
- Business Need: Cross-sell multiple services
- User Need: Quick overview of what you offer
- Action: Increases engagement

**498A - Red Emergency:**
- Color Psychology: Red = urgent, important, help
- Time Sensitivity: Victims need immediate help
- Action: Encourages immediate contact

**Supreme Court - Gold Badge:**
- Color Psychology: Gold = premium, excellence, trust
- Credibility Need: AOR certification is rare
- Action: Builds immediate trust

**Writ/PIL - Comparison Table:**
- Information Need: People confuse writ and PIL
- Visual Learning: Table format is clearest
- Action: Educates before consultation
