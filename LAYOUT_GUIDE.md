## 🎨 Section Types Explained

## 🎨 Color Usage Guide

### Navy (#1a2744)
- Hero background (gradient)
- All headings (H1, H2, H3)
- Form background
- Card icons
- CTA button text

### Gold (#c9a84c)
- CTA buttons (primary action)
- Accent icons (checkmarks, numbers)
- Links on hover
- FAQ indicators
- Border accents
- "How We Help" section background

### White (#ffffff)
- Content cards
- Form inputs (with transparency)
- Main background for cards

### Gray (#f5f5f5)
- Page background
- Alternating card backgrounds
- Subtle borders

## 📏 Spacing Standards

### Section Spacing
- Between major sections: `space-y-12` (48px)
- Inside cards: `p-8` (32px padding)
- Between list items: `space-y-3` or `space-y-4`

### Typography Sizing
- **H1 (Hero):** `text-5xl` (48px)
- **H2 (Section):** `text-3xl` (30px)
- **H3 (Subsection):** `text-xl` or `text-lg`
- **Body:** `text-base` (16px)
- **Small text:** `text-sm` (14px)

### Icons
- Section headers: `size={24}`
- List items: `size={20}`
- Small accents: `size={16}`

## 🎭 Animation & Interactions

### Hover States
- **Cards:** Border color changes to gold, shadow appears
- **Buttons:** Background darkens, shadow increases
- **Links:** Text color changes to gold
- **FAQ Items:** Border color changes to gold

### Animations
- **FAQ Expand:** `slide-down` animation (0.3s ease-out)
- **Form Submit:** Success notification slides in
- **Page Load:** Smooth scroll to top

### Transitions
All transitions: `transition-all` or `transition-colors`  
Duration: Default (200ms)

## 🎯 Design Principles Applied

### 1. Visual Hierarchy
- Largest text = most important (hero title)
- Icons draw attention to section headers
- Gold color highlights key actions/info
- White space separates sections clearly

### 2. Scannability
- Icons make sections quickly identifiable
- Numbered steps are easy to follow
- Checkmarks indicate completeness
- Cards group related information

### 3. Professional Legal Aesthetic
- Serif fonts = traditional, authoritative
- Navy = trustworthy, professional
- Gold = premium, excellence
- Clean layout = organized, competent

### 4. User Experience
- Sticky sidebar = always accessible form
- Accordion FAQs = progressive disclosure
- Breadcrumbs = easy navigation
- Responsive = works on all devices

## 🎬 User Journey

### Typical Visitor Flow:

1. **Lands on page** (from Google/your site)
   - Sees compelling hero immediately
   
2. **Reads about section**
   - Understands what the service is

## 🎨 Customization Quick Reference

### Change Button Text
**File:** `servicesData.js`  
**Line:** `cta: 'Get Free Consultation'`  
**Change to:** `cta: 'Book Now'` or `cta: 'Call Us Today'`

### Change Form Title
**File:** `ConsultationForm.jsx`  
**Line 43:** `Book a Consultation`  
**Change to:** Your preferred text

### Change Section Icons
**File:** `ServiceDetail.jsx`  
**Import more icons from:** `lucide-react`  
**Replace:** `<FileText size={24} />` with your icon

### Change Color Scheme
**Find/Replace in all component files:**
- `#1a2744` → Your navy
- `#c9a84c` → Your gold
- `#f5f5f5` → Your background
